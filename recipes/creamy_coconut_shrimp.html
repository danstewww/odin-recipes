Easy Buffet Style Creamy Chinese Coconut Shrimp Recipe

You'll love this savory but simple Chinese recipe!

This Creamy Chinese Coconut Shrimp Recipe is a quick and easy 30-minute dinner! The silky, smooth, creamy sauce goes amazing with the shrimp and prawns.

To soak up the sauce, serve the dish over rice, quinoa, or creamy mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp(medium to large shrimp)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 green onion(Garnish)
  • 1 cup oil(Frying)

Tempure batter mix:

  • 130 mk chilled soda water or sprinkling water
  • 1 egg
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour or cake flour
  • 3 tables corn starch

Coconut Sauce:

  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons coconut cream
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons corn starch
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

Tempura batter and Frying shrimps

  1. Whisk together egg and chilled soda water. Pour it over flour and corn starch. Keep mixing until all combined. Skip this step if using a store-bought tempura batter.
  2. Add shrimp to a large bowl, and sprinkle with salt and black pepper. Pour tempura batter on the shrimp; make sure every shrimp is coated with the batter.
  3. Add oil to a large skillet on medium to high heat. Fry battered shrimp on each side for 3-4 minutes until crispy.
  4. Place fried shrimp on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

Coconut Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, coconut cream, sweetened condensed milk, mayonnaise, corn starch, and salt. Mix until all combined.
  2. Cook the coconut sauce on medium heat while constantly whisking until smooth and thick.

Assembly

  1. Place fried shrimp in a large bowl, pour coconut sauce on top, and mix until shrimp are coated with the sauce.
  2. Garnish with green onions, and enjoy!
